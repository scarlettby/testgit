Discover Center Parcs forest holidays

Welcome to Center Parcs, the award-winning number one short break provider in the UK. Our five Villages are nestled within beautiful and secluded woodland, providing an ideal location for a unique short break to remember. Whether you want to zip wire through the forest, canoe along the calm of our lakes, or you simply want to relax and soak up the natural beauty, every short break at Center Parcs is tailored to you.


The great thing about staying in our forests is that you’re surrounded by the flora and fauna all year round. We offer a number of nature activities; meet a Ranger on Wildlife Walks, spot some feathered friends during Family Bird Watching, or explore the forest before anyone else during Woodland Awakening. Our extensive range of sports and leisure activities, as well as our Subtropical Swimming Paradise, means there’s something to keep the whole family entertained, whatever the weather.

 

Making the most of your break

Subtropical Swimming Paradise ►

Subtropical Swimming Paradise

Nestled in the heart of our forests is the Subtropical Swimming Paradise. Open daily from 10.00am to 9.00pm and included within the cost of your break there is fun for all the family. With family and toddler water playgrounds, waterslides, flumes, exciting rapids, hot whirlpools and more you can just turn up and enjoy. This idyllic setting is heated to 29.5°C whatever the weather, so you can wander beneath the palms, swim in deliciously warm waters or sip a cocktail at the Canopy Café and Bar.

 

Reassuring Breaks for Children ►

Reassuring Breaks for Children

Whatever the age, Center Parcs has a range of activities for children. There’s something fresh and exciting to discover every day: a new skill to master, like swimming or riding a bike; a new activity, like Aqua Jetting; a new wonder, like seeing the wildlife outside your patio door. We’ve gone the extra mile to smooth the way for families.

We are proud to have been awarded the Tommy’s Baby Friendly Award for ten consecutive years.



Actively Inspired ►

Actively Inspired

Center Parcs is not just for children. Grown ups, you can be on the go all day too or as laid back as you like. At Center Parcs there is no pressure; it’s up to you to decide. What we can promise are lots of possibilities from calm creative activities, to demanding competitive challenges to get your heart racing. Indoor or outdoor, wet or dry, you’ll find plenty of scope to enjoy yourselves.

Relaxation ►

Relaxation

Time melts away at Aqua Sana, our very own spas. Slow down and relax completely. With over 15 experience rooms, an outdoor pool and a wide range of treatments, as well as delicious Mediterranean cuisine at Vitalé Café Bar, it’s easier than ever to let go and discover a renewed, radiant you.

Getting Around ►

Getting Around

The best way to get around our Villages is by bike. We have up to 400 acres of forest each with plenty of cycle paths to explore. To help preserve a safe and tranquil environment, car access to the Villages is strictly limited making Center Parcs a safe place for your little ones to explore on two wheels. You’re welcome to bring your own bikes or save the hassle and hire them through the Cycle Centre. You can hire a variety of bikes as well as child seats, trailers, speciality cycles and mobility scooters.

Booking information

We are open all year round

We are open 365 days of the year and operate short breaks; a three night weekend break, a four night midweek break as well as a seven night break, which you can select when making your search or booking.

Within the booking process you can select a location preference or a specific Lodge. We provide the option of specially adapted Lodges and Lodges designed especially for dogs, simply select ‘yes’ for additional requirements when making your search.

What’s included in your break

The price of your break includes your accommodation and full use of our stunning forest surroundings including the beachfront, children’s adventure play areas and our jewel in the crown, the Subtropical Swimming Paradise.

Your arrival day

You can arrive anytime from 10am to make the most of your first day. On check-in at the Arrivals Lodge you will receive your Lodge keys and a car security pass. If you arrive before 3pm by car, you must leave it in the car park but you are welcome to use the facilities throughout the day. Entry into your accommodation is from 3pm for all Villages except Whinfell Forest which is 3:30pm. You can drive to your accommodation to unload, before returning your car to the car park for the remainder of your break.

Locations

We have five beautiful forest locations to choose from making all that Center Parcs has to offer more easily accessible for all families within the UK.

Whinfell Forest
Cumbria

Nestled in a stunning landscape on the edge of the Lake District and a wildlife haven, home to our growing population of endangered Red Squirrels.

Sherwood Forest
Nottinghamshire

Set in the deep Nottinghamshire countryside and legendary Robin Hood county, there’s an invigorating mix of streams, lakes and woodland.

Elveden Forest
Suffolk

Set in over 400 acres of stunning forest in delightful rural Suffolk and home to our award winning, gravity defying water ride, Tropical Cyclone.

Longleat Forest
Wiltshire

Situated in the beautiful Longleat Estate. The hilly landscape is truly spectacular and with hundred-year-old Giant Redwoods, you’ll be delighted with the setting.

Woburn Forest
Bedfordshire

Only an hour from London and nestled in a 365 acre forest, our new Village, Woburn Forest will be the showcase of our latest innovations.
